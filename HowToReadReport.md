# How to read the email

## Signal

- **BUY ZONE:** spot is at least 5% below the 50-day average.
- **WATCH:** spot is below the 50-day average but has not reached the 5% threshold.
- **WAIT:** spot is at or above the 50-day average.

This is a transparent rule, not a promise that price will rise or fall.

## Market snapshot

- **Spot AUD/CNY per gram:** the latest gold futures close converted using current FX rates.
- **Daily move:** movement between the two latest gold closes.
- **50-day average:** medium-term trend context.
- **200-day average:** long-term trend context.

## Retail premium

Perth Mint prices are compared with the spot value of the gold content. A positive premium covers fabrication, distribution, brand value, and retail margin. Scraped prices are marked unavailable instead of guessed when the store page changes or blocks a request.

## Store status

Status uses `Australia/Perth` local time, trading hours, weekends, and the configured WA holiday calendar. Confirm hours with the Perth Mint before travelling.

## Limitations

Gold futures are a liquid market proxy, not the exact physical-gold transaction price. FX rates, retail prices, and store availability can move between the report and a purchase. The report is not financial advice.
